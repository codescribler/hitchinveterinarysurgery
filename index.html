Our Services

Expert care across every stage of your pet's life

General Consultations & Health Checks

Whether your pet is poorly, needs a health check, or you're just worried about something, our vets offer thorough consultations with detailed explanations. Routine vaccinations, flea/worm treatments, microchipping, and preventative care. Think of us as your first call.

Advanced Surgery (Orthopaedic & Soft Tissue)

Complex fractures, joint problems, ligament tears, internal injuries — these need precision and experience. Our directors hold advanced certifications in orthopaedics. We perform TPLO surgery, laparoscopic keyhole surgery, and soft tissue surgery. Many cases that would once need referral can now be handled here.

Emergency & Out-of-Hours Care

Pets don't get ill on a schedule. If your pet is injured or in distress outside standard hours, we have emergency appointment slots for trauma, acute illness, and urgent pain management.

Cat Friendly Clinic

Cats are not small dogs. We're certified Cat Friendly, meaning low-stress handling, quiet consultation rooms, and vets who understand feline behaviour. If you have an anxious cat, we know how to help.

Dentistry, Radiography & Ultrasound

Dental disease is silent and common. Professional cleaning, extractions, full mouth digital radiography (award-winning setup), and ultrasound for internal diagnosis — all in-house.

Nurse Clinics & Preventative Care

Our experienced nurses run wellness clinics for vaccinations, health checks, weight management, and behavioural advice. Prevention is always cheaper and kinder than cure.

The Risk of Waiting

What Happens If You Delay

Minor issues become major ones

Delaying a health check can turn a treatable problem into a serious one. An infection gets worse. A joint problem leads to chronic pain. A lump that could have been caught early becomes untreatable. Prevention costs pennies compared to emergency treatment.

The wrong vet costs more than money

If you choose a vet based on convenience alone, you might end up with unnecessary treatments, poor communication, or a vet who doesn't listen. You'll question every recommendation. You'll feel uncertain when your pet really needs you to advocate for them.

Your pet suffers longer than necessary

Your pet can't tell you how much pain they're in. Early diagnosis and treatment is the kindest thing you can do for them. Don't wait until there's a crisis. Book a check-up today and give them the care they deserve.
